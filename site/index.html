How it works

Your agent asks a question. BookLab returns ranked, opinionated recommendations — not generic lists, but genuine human curation.

→
"I feel lost and need to find meaning"
→ Man's Search for Meaning, Flow, The Denial of Death
→
"Why do smart people believe stupid things?"
→ The Righteous Mind, The True Believer, Spiral Dynamics
→
"Teach me about human civilization"
→ Sapiens, The Better Angels of Our Nature, The Beginning of Infinity

What your agent gets

Three tools and a full library resource, all via the Model Context Protocol.

🔍

recommend

Semantic search across themes, insights, and emotional tone. Describe a situation, get ranked picks.

📖

book_profile

Full structured profile — themes, worldview, difficulty, connections between books, and Bjorn's honest take.

📚

list_books

Browse the complete curated library with ratings, tags, and categories at a glance.

🧠

Opinionated

Not Amazon ratings. Real opinions from a decade of reading 30+ nonfiction books per year.

Why BookLab?

Agents are born smart but uncultured. They have training data but lack curated, opinionated, structured wisdom about books. BookLab has 10+ years of exactly that.

Built by Björn Röjgren — creator of BookLab by Bjorn (16k YouTube subscribers, 43k X followers). Reading, reviewing, and recommending the best nonfiction since 2015.
